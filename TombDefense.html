Itch.io Meta Quest

Tomb Defense is a VR arcade shooter built for Meta Quest in Unreal Engine 5

The player must shoot through hordes of the undead to get a highscore. Throughout the game the player can use powerups to gain the advantage ranging from extra health to epic slo-mo action.
