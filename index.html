About Me

Hello, I am Amy Cooper. I am a games design student in my final year studying Games Design and Production Bsc (Hons) at University of Staffordshire. I have designed and developed a variety of games from walking sims to arcade action throughout my studies and in personal projects. I specialise in Unreal Engine using blueprint. My primary interests are in building arcade experiences and virtual reality worlds.

This site is under construction. Not all my projects are listed here yet and more will be added soon.
